Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduRahisi?

EduRahisi is an online learning platform that offers a variety of courses to help learners enhance their skills and knowledge.

How can I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply visit the Courses page, select the course you are interested in, and follow the enrollment instructions.

Is there a fee for courses?

Some courses on EduRahisi are free, while others may require a fee. Please check the course details for more information.

Can I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you will receive a certificate that you can share with potential employers or educational institutions.

What subjects are available?

EduRahisi offers a wide range of subjects, including Mathematics, Coding, English Language, and Science. You can explore the full list of courses on our Courses page.

How can I access the courses?

You can access the courses by creating an account on our platform. Once registered, you can log in and start exploring the available courses.

Do I need any special software to take the courses?

No special software is required to take the courses. You can access them through any web browser on your computer or mobile device.
